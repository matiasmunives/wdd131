Overview

Site Name

The site name is "M&M Facilities Management".
The name was selected following two keys:
- First one is my personal name "Matías Munives" represented in the initials "M&M".
- Second one is the services of the company "Facilities Services".

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to offer Facilities Management Services for companies.
These services are design and management of building maintenance and construction, maintenance services as surfaces painting, plumbing, gardening, electricity, general repairs, and others.

Scenarios

-Where can I find contact information of the company?
Go to the "About Us" section and there all the company's info will be deployed.
-How can I hire the company's services?
In the "About us" section complete the form and we will contact you. Also, you can call us using the phone numbers of the company's information.
-What services I can hire?
The company provide building projects, maintenances services, and general repairs. In the services section more info about the services is detailed.
Go to the "About Us" section and there all the company's info will be deployed.

Typography

Heading Font: Roboto

Roboto font from Google Font is selected for the heading because this font is a formal, elegant, professional, and non-classic typeface, which perfectly matches the purpose of the website and the company. It's easy to read, it's reliable, and it's eye-catching. It is a san-serif font.

Paragraph Font: Nunito Sans

Nunito Sans font from Google Font is selected because it combines well with the font of the headings. This font has a sober style, similar to classic fonts, but with some distinctive curves that make a difference and give it modernity. In addition, this font is easy to read, legible, easy on the eyes, and adds a touch of professionalism to the website. The headlines demonstrate the company's style, and the paragraphs give professionalism.
