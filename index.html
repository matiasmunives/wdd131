Córdoba, Argentina

Argentina is a large South American country with diverse terrain that includes the Andes mountains, glacial lakes, and the Pampas grasslands, which are the traditional grazing lands for its famous cattle. The country is renowned for the tango dance and music. Its large, cosmopolitan capital, Buenos Aires, is centered around the Plaza de Mayo, which is surrounded by impressive 19th-century buildings, including the Casa Rosada, the iconic presidential palace.
